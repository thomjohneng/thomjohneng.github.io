EATING

EATING

If we're not on this earth to eat then I'm not sure what we're doing here. Eating is one of my greatest passions and I'd be nothing without it.

I am particularly fond of the exquisite simplicity of Japanese cuisine, the heavy succulence of Italian fare, and (sticking to my roots) hearty, saucy Chinese delicacies.

I'm always on the lookout for creative restaurants exploring cross-cuisine gastronomy, hunting for the best NYC pizza, and obviously digging my hands in a basket of good ol' fried chicken.

DIVERSITY

DIVERSITY

Diversity simply means variety and having a range of different things. Some people fear diversity (see this), and some people praise it as a necessity (see this).

In my mind, diversity is a key characteristic of our humanity and should be an important consideration when we talk about the future. To be completely clear here (because some people have trouble defining what diversity means), I'm talking about diversity of cultures & ethnicities, socio-economic backgrounds, gender & sex, religion and political opinion, etc. ~ you name it! When thinking about the future of our world, we should be inclusive of everyone whom it houses.

EDUCATION

EDUCATION

It's surprising to me that only a portion of the world's population has access to formal education (see this). I feel that education is integral in our pursuit of progress. Building a strong society and economy all starts with proper learning.

I want to also highlight the idea of continuous learning. At the rate that society progresses and technology develops, things that we know today will become obsolete in 5 years. I am always trying to learn new things that interest me in order to keep my mind curious.

SUSTAINABILITY

SUSTAINABILITY

From an environmental perspective, we only have this one Earth (for now) and I believe we should treat it with as much respect as we expect it to treat us. It might sound corny, but if we love and cherish this planet, it will love and cherish our time on it too :)

But sustainability goes beyond the environment and the world we live in. When I say sustainability I'm talking about building something that's can have sustainable impact. I'm thinking about the continuation of life beyond just it's habitat. Take a look at this. I want to leave this world knowing that I've tried my best to contribute to these four pillars of sustainability.

HEALTH & WELLNESS

HEALTH & WELLNESS

I think it's essential that we care for ourselves properly in terms of the food we eat and the things we do. I always look to treat myself with good grub and I try my best to stay active.

Additionally, the concept of work-life balance and work-life symbiosis is important to me. I will always strive to keep a balance between work and play and I won't hesitate to draw the line between the two. It helps when I'm doing what I love and loving what I do :)
